About School Page


Learning to navigate unfamiliar environments has always been a key part of the Smart Academy. Be it inculcating leadership qualities through simulating healthy and challenging situations or providing the best IIT foundation. We want our kids to look at the unknown situation and enjoy the experience of overcoming the obstacles making it a Known situation. And we try to bring it as close as possible to the real-world scenario to spike our kid's interest.


Our Values


VISON: Education enriches the society, broadens its boundaries, and creates intellectual thinkers above and beyond mere literates. The conventional methods of teaching are limited to just grades and accolades, completely missing the prime objectives of imparting education. Four visionaries back in 1988, set up an institution that will change the meaning of education through the holistic approach. Smart Academy has since then become the benchmark of a complete education, producing individuals who know more than knowledge and think more than thoughts..

MISSION: Each individual’s academic, emotional, social, physical, creative and spiritual potentials are to be cultured in moving towards the highest aspirations of the human spirit.
